### **Camera Usage**
*   **Key:** `NSCameraUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Optimal Protocol requires camera access to capture photographic proof of your habit completion. Images are processed locally to generate your status overlay."

*   **Key:** `NSUserNotificationsUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Optimal Protocol uses notifications to issue morning directives and evening status checks. Compliance is mandatory for maintaining your streak."

### **Photo Library Add**
*   **Key:** `NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription`
*   **Value:** "Required to save your 'Proof of Work' overlays to your device for sharing."
