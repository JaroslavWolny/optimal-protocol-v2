# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: November 30, 2025**

## 1. INTRODUCTION
**Optimal Protocol** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Optimal Protocol.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website, and its associated subdomains (collectively, our "Service") alongside our application, Optimal Protocol. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### A. Personal Data
When you register for the Service, we collect:
-   **Email Address:** Used for authentication (Magic Link) and account recovery.
-   **User ID:** A unique identifier generated by our authentication provider (Supabase).

### B. Usage Data
We collect data generated by your use of the app:
-   **Habit Logs:** Titles, categories, and completion timestamps of the habits you track.
-   **Game Stats:** Your streak, level, and avatar configuration.
-   **Device Information:** We may collect information about the device you use to access the Service (model, OS version) for debugging purposes.

### C. Camera & Photos
-   **Camera:** We use the camera ONLY to allow you to take photos for "Proof of Work" overlays. These photos are processed locally on your device or uploaded to your private storage bucket if you explicitly choose to save them.
-   **Photo Library:** We request write-only access to save generated images (Share Cards) to your camera roll.

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:
-   Provide, operate, and maintain our Service.
-   Improve, personalize, and expand our Service.
-   Understand and analyze how you use our Service.
-   Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
-   Send you emails (Magic Links) for authentication.

## 4. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY
We use **Supabase** (a secure, open-source Firebase alternative) to store your data.
-   **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest.
-   **Access Control:** We implement Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that you can only access and modify your own data.

## 5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
-   **Supabase:** For database and authentication services.
-   **Vercel:** For web hosting (if applicable).

## 6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR/CCPA)
You have the right to:
-   Access the personal data we hold about you.
-   Request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
-   Request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten"). You can delete your account directly within the app settings.

## 7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
Optimal Protocol does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.
